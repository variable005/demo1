FAQ / DECRYPTED

Common queries, direct answers.

Who can participate?

All enrolled university students are eligible. Teams of 1-2 operators. Solo participants can find a teammate during the initial phase.

What should I bring?

Your primary workstation (laptop), charger, and any specialized peripherals. Stable network access and power units will be provided.

Is coding experience required?

No formal pre-requisites. A basic understanding of coding is beneficial, but enthusiasm and a willingness to learn are paramount.

Will food be provided?

Affirmative. Nutritional support and hydration stations will be available throughout the operational period.
